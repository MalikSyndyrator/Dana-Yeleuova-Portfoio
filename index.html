Dana

Graphic Designer & AI Creator

I am a Graphic Designer and AI creator based in Florence, working globally across brand identity, advertising campaigns, editorial design and bespoke digital experiences.

I create visual systems that bring clarity, structure and emotional presence to a brand. My work is not only about aesthetics, but about building experiences that stay in people’s minds: through identity, layout, storytelling, art direction and campaign visuals.

With a multicultural background and experience across international creative environments, I approach each project with a wide visual perspective and a strong sense of context. My approach combines graphic design, art direction and AI-assisted visual production to create distinctive identities, campaign concepts and digital content for brands that want to stand out with intention.
